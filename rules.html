Rules

We can’t have an excellent service without a good set of rules that our users must follow in order to ensure that we’re providing a good service.

We can perfectly promise you that:

  • We will not look at your passwords.
  • We will not spam your e-mail addresses.

However, you need to promise that you will use your bouncer following these rules so that we can trust you as a good user.

  • Don’t use your bouncer for illegal stuff!
  • Make sure to log in at least once every thirty days.
  • Don’t connect to your bouncer through a proxy or another bouncer.
  • Spamming/Flooding while using your bouncer is not allowed.
  • We deserve the rights to remove your bouncer with any or no reason.
  • Do not change the networks the bouncer is connected to. Please inform us if you want to change your networks.
  • Having multiple accounts on the same network is not allowed.
  • Do not create more than one DreamBNC account.

With those rules mentioned, you will also have to follow these terms and conditions regarding the networks where you will use your bouncer.

  • Don’t break any of your networks rules while using your bouncer.
  • Network staff can request us to remove or block your account.

Any network ban will get your account removed, and you will then be banned from DreamBNC, temporarily or permanently depending on the network ban type and reason.

And not to forget to mention, that we have the rights to change any of these rules at any moment, with or without any notice.
