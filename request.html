Request

There are different ways for you to request a bouncer on DreamBNC. However, you must make sure that you will follow the rules before requesting.

  • IRC Message: Connect to Rizon and send a message to our channel with your request details: /msg #DreamBNC-Staff request username network port e-mail
  • E-mail: Send an email to Support@DreamBNC.XYZ with your account details (username, network and port)
